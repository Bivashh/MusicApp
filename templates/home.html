About Narajyoti

Guided growth,
note by note.

Narajyoti Music School was built around one belief — that every student grows when given the right structure, honest feedback, and an encouraging space to learn. We focus on steady, visible improvement in technique, rhythm, and performance confidence.

"The goal is not perfection — it is consistent, joyful progress."

✅
Structured Learning
Clear lesson plans and practice routines so students always know what's next.
🎤
Performance Focus
Build the confidence to sing and play in front of others, step by step.
🧠
Helpful Feedback
Precise guidance on pitch, rhythm, and technique — not just encouragement.
🤝
Supportive Environment
Friendly teaching with steady encouragement from day one.
Courses

Pick your path.

Every course is built around clear progression — so you always know where you're headed.

🎤
01 — Vocals
Vocal Training
Breath control, pitch accuracy, tone improvement, and stage confidence.
🎸
02 — Strings
Guitar
Chords, strumming patterns, rhythm control, and song practice from the ground up.
🎹
03 — Keys
Keyboard / Harmonium
Notes, scales, accompaniment, and foundational music theory.
🥁
04 — Rhythm
Rhythm Practice
Timing, claps, counts, and exercises to strengthen musical accuracy.
🎶
05 — Performance
Song Coaching
Expression, stage readiness, and performance confidence building.
📚
06 — Foundation
Beginner Foundation
Basics, posture, practice habits, and progress tracking for new learners.
